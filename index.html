Platformer Quest

Platformer Quest is a challenging platformer game where timing and precision is key. There are obstacles and tricky jumps along the way, and if you die, you restart from the beginning. Other Scratch games and local online platforms inspired this game, especially Jason Erdreich's "Programming a Retro-Style Video Game in P5.js" series.

By: Zanroe and Jeremiah
